Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a court?

Browse available courts in the Courts tab, select your preferred court, choose a time slot, and tap "Book Now". You'll receive a confirmation once your booking is complete.

How do I become a facility owner?

You can add your facility through the app by going to Profile > Switch to Owner. Fill in your facility details, upload photos, and your court will be available for bookings after approval.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes! Go to your Bookings tab, select the booking you want to cancel, and tap "Cancel Booking". Please note cancellation policies may vary by facility.

How do game invitations work?

After booking a court, you can invite friends to join your game. They'll receive a notification and can accept or decline the invitation. All participants will see the game details in their bookings.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! Sporty takes your privacy seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Why isn't location working?

Make sure you've granted location permissions to Sporty in your device settings. Go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Sporty and select "While Using the App".
