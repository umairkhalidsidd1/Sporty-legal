5. Booking Policies

5.1 Booking Confirmation

When you make a booking through Sporty:

  • You enter into a direct contract with the court owner
  • Sporty acts as a platform facilitating the transaction
  • Bookings are subject to court owner acceptance (for request-based bookings)
  • You will receive confirmation via email, push notification, or in-app notification

5.2 Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation policies vary by court and are set by individual court owners. Generally:

  • Cancellations made 24+ hours before booking time may receive a full or partial refund
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before booking may not be refundable
  • No-shows typically result in forfeiture of payment
  • Weather-related cancellations are subject to court owner policies
  • Emergency cancellations will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis

Specific cancellation terms will be displayed before you complete your booking.

5.3 Payment Terms

  • Payment is processed at the time of booking or as specified by the court owner
  • All prices are displayed in the local currency
  • Sporty may charge a service fee or commission on bookings
  • You are responsible for any applicable taxes
  • Refunds (when applicable) will be processed to your original payment method

5.4 Booking Disputes

If you have a dispute regarding a booking:

  • Contact the court owner directly through the Application
  • If unresolved, contact Sporty customer support at kicksetgoo@gmail.com
  • Provide relevant details (booking ID, date, issue description)

  • Final decisions on refunds rest with Sporty's discretion
